Frequently Asked Questions

What are your shipping options?

We offer standard shipping (3-5 business days), express shipping (2-3 business days), and overnight shipping. All orders over $50 qualify for free standard shipping.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Products must be unused and in original packaging. Certain items like opened software or custom products may not be eligible for return.

Do you offer warranty on products?

Yes, all products come with manufacturer warranty. The warranty period varies by product and brand. Extended warranty options are available at checkout.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order by logging into your account and viewing your order history.

Do you have a physical store?

Yes, we have a flagship store in Silicon Valley. Visit us at 123 Tech Street during business hours to see our products in person and get expert advice from our team.
